Machine Learning Models

What are they?

Machine Learning models are algorithms that can learn from and make predictions or decisions based on data. Examples include Decision Tree Classifier and Linear Regression.

Why do we use them?

We use these models to make predictions, classifications, or decisions based on the dataset. They help us automate the analysis process and provide insights that would be difficult to obtain manually.

How do they affect our dataset?

Machine Learning models transform the dataset by identifying relationships and patterns within the data. These transformations can lead to more accurate predictions and a deeper understanding of the dataset's underlying structure.
